I Am The Lord your Healer

God is the healer and he has revealed to us ways, how to get healed, he has given us mastery over illness in Matthew 10:5-8. As Christians when we hear someone is sick, we are obliged to know what to do by affirming our healing in Christ Jesus. If a colleague or a neighbour is sick, we should get them healed by proclaiming our authority over sicknesses and diseases and also engaging our faith with theirs.
Here is a deeper understanding I received from the Holy Spirit, that if someone is sick we can get them healed by bringing them into our reality. By way of illustration, our reality as Christians, that no sickness has authority over us. Hence, when we inquire someone to declare that 'they are healed and believe that they are healed' then we are bringing them into our reality and our world of different dimensions of healing.
In this book, you will be shown those ways about the authority which God has given to us and how we can emphasize it healthwise.
